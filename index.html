Get ready to embark on a classic gaming adventure.


♦ Objective: Control Pacman with arrow keys to eat all the dots and avoid the ghosts.
♦ Controls: Use arrow keys (up, down, left, right) to move Pacman. To pause or resume game, use spacebar.
♦ Gameplay: Eat dots for points, avoid ghosts. Power pellets make Pacman temporarily invincible.
♦ Scoring: Earn points by eating dots, power pellets, and ghosts.
♦ Game Over: Win by eating all the dots or lose when ghost eats character.


Enjoy the challenge and have a great time playing Pacman!
